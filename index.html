How to Find Student Accommodation with Pekugara

For Students

  1. Create an account – Sign up with your university email in under a minute.
  2. Search listings – Browse hundreds of verified student rooms and flats near your campus.
  3. Pay the booking fee – Pay a small $2 fee via EcoCash to unlock messaging and book a viewing.
  4. Move in – Confirm your viewing, get accepted by the landlord, and move into your new home.

For Landlords

  1. Create a landlord account – Register and get verified by our admin team.
  2. List your property – Add photos, amenities, price and location. Goes live after approval.
  3. Receive booking requests – Students pay a booking fee and send you a viewing request.
  4. Confirm your tenant – Review student profiles, confirm the best match, and finalize the arrangement.

About Pekugara – Zimbabwe's Student Housing Platform

Finding student accommodation in Zimbabwe is hard. Listings are scattered across WhatsApp groups, Facebook pages, and word of mouth — making it stressful, slow, and often unsafe for students.

The name Pekugara means "a place to stay" in Shona. That's exactly what we're here to help every Zimbabwean student find.

Founded in 2025 in Harare, Zimbabwe, Pekugara launched on the iOS App Store in 2026 with over 500 listings across 10 cities and more than 2,000 students already finding housing through the platform.

Our Mission

Make student housing in Zimbabwe accessible, transparent, and safe. Every student deserves a secure, affordable place to stay near their university — without the stress of unverified landlords and scattered listings.

Our Vision

Become the leading student housing platform across Southern Africa, expanding from Zimbabwe into Zambia, Mozambique, and beyond.
